# Bertran-Gordon Deed, Dec. 22, 1875

Source: https://www.fromthepage.com/highpointmuseum/high-point-archives-project/work-1996-019-023

The following text is the public FromThePage transcription visible on the source work page.

## Page 1

Deed  
Daniel Bertran  
& wife  
To  
A. A. Gordon (widower)

Mail A. A. Gordon  
High Point  
Route 1  
Record Pd. 1.00

## Page 2

THIS DEED, Made this 22 day of Dec. 1875  
by Daniel Bertran and wife Sarah Jane  
of Guilford County, and State of North Carolina  
to Alfred A. Gordon of the same  
County, and State.

ATTEST:  
[blank]

## Page 3

State of North Carolina,  
Guilford County.  
IN PROBATE COURT.

J. N. Nelson  
Probate Judge.
